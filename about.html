New Features

AR Tracking

In the latest version of My Buddy, several new features have been implemented, including an unlock system of new food items via augmented reality. This system uses fiducial marker tracking to detect if specific patterns are present, with these specific patterns being unlock keys for several different new food items! Get 'em while food is free; in the next update, currency (paying for your pet's food) should be implemented!

If you want these patterns, the files are available publicly on the GitHub Page. Feel free to download them and scan them in from your phone!

Evolutions

Finally, there are some new characters that we can welcome to our lineup! This update introduces two new evolutions from the base character, our favorite blue blob. These characters can be attained after you raise your Buddy for one day, a year in their time, and will be picked depending on how well you cared for your Buddy!

Our first character is a little lightbulb who's just looking to brighten someone's day. You've gotta take really good care of your Buddy if you want to get a chance to get to raise this character!

Uh oh...if you neglect your first buddy, you could end up with this mischevious character! They smile when they tell you no, and overall seem to always be causing drama and sulking around. That is, except when they can get a bite to eat, or if they're feeling particularly affectionate with you. It's not just a phase, mom!
